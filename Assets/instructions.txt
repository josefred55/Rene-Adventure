Guide Rene trought the colorful world!, Collect coins, powerUps, collectibles, 
defeat enemies and more awaits you in this adventure.

- Move with arrow keys or WASD.

- Jump with W or up key.

- When having a fire powerUp, shoot fireballs with Z.
You can kill enemies shooting fireballs at them.

- Jump into a wall or a platform by it's side and move to the direction of the wall to slide in it,
then, press jump to perform a wall jump.

- Grab hearts to recover life if you took damage.
